Membership

Joining Spectra

We have established 3 tiers of membership:

  • Individual
  • Academic Institutions
  • Corporate

For this first year, everyone on the mailing list will be counted as members. You can also email the Membership Committee Chair (Joseph Nakao).

Individual Memberships

Annual Cost: $0

Individual members are able to vote in Spectra elections.

Academic Institution Membership

Cost: $100 annually

Academic Institutions Members are asked to affirm the following statement: "As an institutional/corporate member of Spectra, we pledge to support and encourage LGBTQ+ individuals in mathematics, by working to create an inclusive and affirming environment that supports the well-being and professional development of LGBTQ+ mathematicians. We join the work creating a time when LGBTQ+ mathematicians are able to bring their whole selves to the mathematical community."

We will have a dedicated page providing information for Academic Institutions which are members.

Corporate Memberships

Cost: $200 annually

Corporate Members are also asked to affirm the following statement: "As an institutional/corporate member of Spectra, we pledge to support and encourage LGBTQ+ individuals in mathematics, by working to create an inclusive and affirming environment that supports the well-being and professional development of LGBTQ+ mathematicians. We join the work creating a time when LGBTQ+ mathematicians are able to bring their whole selves to the mathematical community."

We will have a dedicated page providing information for Corporate Members.

Student Chapters

Spectra is delight to support students wanting to form Spectra Student Chapters at their home institutions. The set-up is pretty simple, and it is outlined in the Welcome Document. If you have any questions, please reach out to the Membership Chair.

Forming Student Chapters

To form a Spectra chapter, you will need to send the Membership Chair two things:

  • please fill out the Application Form.
  • You will need to craft and include with your application your proposed bylaws; here is a bylaws template.

After receipt of your application materials, the Membership Chair will bring your application to the next Board Meeting. You should hear shortly thereafter! There is no cost associated with forming or maintaining a Spectra student chapter.

If you need to update your bylaws, change officers or faculty advisor, or dissolve your chapter, please fill out the Update Form.

Supporting Student Chapters

Spectra student chapters in good standing have a few annual membership requirements:

  • Submit to the Membership Chair an annual report form detailing chapter events.
  • Keep the Student Chapter Membership spreadsheet which is shared with the Membership Chair up-to-date to be sure your members are all listed as Spectra Individual Members
