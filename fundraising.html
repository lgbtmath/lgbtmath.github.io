Join our Fundraising Campaign!

Our organization has grown tremendously over the last several years, and we have a ton of planned activities and efforts that need your support. We are working to build an endowment to ensure that Spectra is able to continue supporting our community for years to come.

Here are some of our goals:

  • To continue to build a robust academic and social presence at major professional society meetings from the JMM to MathFest and SIAM events.
  • Lift up members of our community working in theoretical math, applied math, and math eduation, showcasing the incredible talent of our community through the Lavender Lecture.
  • Support early career mathematicians, especially the students building community through our student chapters.
